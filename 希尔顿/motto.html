Quality Eats

A Well-Designed Sleep

We packed thoughtfully efficient spaces with beautiful design and clever comforts like a top-notch mattress, curated sleep kit, and sound absorbing materials that deliver a quality sleep every time.

The Rooms

Our small but clever rooms are designed with everything you need, including a working surface, vanity, wardrobe, safe, and mini-fridge, with integrated lighting and outlets throughout. Rooms come with flexible furniture and the option of linking doors, so you can create the ideal room combination for you or your group when you book.

Tech-Enabled Rooms

Guests can use their phones to stream preferred entertainment, control TV channels, climate, and lighting through the Hilton Honors app.
